### EP30_Q1
- 기능 / 길이: 충격흡수·절망 진입 underscore / 12–18초
- Lyrics 탭: Write
- Write(구조 태그):
  [Intro]
  [soft]
  [silence seeps into sound]
  [single note, slow attack]
  [Outro]
  [dissolve to stillness]
- Title: EP30_Q1 — Hollow Silence
- Styles: 52 BPM, D minor, minimal emotional neoclassical, muted prepared piano repeating sparse single notes, ultra-thin string pad barely audible, vast reverb tail, cold empty texture, no melody, no dynamic rise, no percussion, no bass movement, no orchestral swell, no resolution, open unresolved harmonic space, fragile and hollow, grief absorbed inward, instrumental, no vocals
- 메모: 도원 "죄송합니다" 직후 1–2초 완전 무음 확보 후 큐 인. 피아노 단음이 물처럼 스며드는 속도가 핵심 — 공격감 없이. 편집 단계에서 볼륨 -10dB 이하로 낮추어 사용.
